Device #: RA-004

Device type: Mobile Platform

Name: Flone 3.0

License: CC-BY-SA

Build Complexity: RA-004 requires a laser cutter, plywood glue, a computer, a soldering iron, and a phone. A 3D printer may be used instead of a laser cutter but that would take additional effort. As most components are built with machines, there is little fine detail work.

RA-004 is a simple project designed with beginers in mind and it isn’t expected to take very long to build. The BoM is approximately 150USD (2016). It is important to note that RA-004 is 9 years old as of writing, thus it is fully expected that many components are no longer available or have changed. This adds a significant level of difficulty, although it can also be considered a learning opportunity for electronic control systems.

Description: RA-004 is a small aproximately 20cm by 20cm camera drone, which is primarily made from plywood. The design does not contain a camera, but rather it has a mount so a phone with the camera facing down can be attached to it. RA-004 is easily modifiable. Example modifications have been created, they are: a gimbaled camera, a seed drop-release system, and a spray paint addon.

RA-004 was well received online when it was created by aeracoop in 2016.
