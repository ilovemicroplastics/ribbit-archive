Curated OSH hardware list

Open Source Hardware is a great way to share designs of devices/robots. The aim of this list is to help share devices which can realistically be built and operated by a single person, especially if: they are suitable as lab instruments, for small scale local production, mobile/stationary robots to mediate the functionality, or home automation. A secondary purpose is to make OSH easier to reference.

This is an overview website. All devices are created by their respective designers, and the device’s precise licensing terms are listed in each document.
